# Aura

Your personal AI that runs entirely on your computer. No cloud dependency. No monthly fees. Your data stays with you.

## Why Aura?

**Private.** Every conversation stays on your machine. No servers, no logs, no privacy policy needed.

**Yours.** Aura learns how *you* write and think. The more you use it, the better it gets at sounding like you.

**Free.** Runs on your laptop — no GPU required. No subscriptions, no tokens, no usage caps.

**Premium when you want it.** Need more power? Unlock cloud models for $10-15/month. Same interface, just faster.

## What it does

- Chat with an AI that remembers your style, preferences, and history
- Works offline — no internet needed for the free tier
- Desktop app for Mac (Windows/Linux coming)

## Getting Started

```bash
bash serve.sh --port 8081
```

Then connect at `http://127.0.0.1:8081`. OpenAI-compatible API.

## Capabilities

- **Multi-Model:** Switch between local (Ollama) and cloud (OpenAI, Gemini) at runtime
- **Computer Control:** See your screen, click, type, drag, press keys, run scripts
- **Video Editing:** Automate DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, and CapCut — import media, edit, export finished files
- **MCP Connectors:** Plug into GitHub, Drive, Slack, and any MCP-compatible service
- **Agent Orchestration:** Lead agent decomposes complex tasks and delegates to sub-agents
- **Per-User Memory:** Remembers your style and conversation history

## Status

Active development. Local tier running on GPT-2; custom model training in progress for better quality without sacrificing speed.
